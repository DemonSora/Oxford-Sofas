About Oxford Sofas

At Oxford Sofas, we believe a sofa is more than just a piece of furniture, it's the heart of a space. It's where conversations happen, comfort is found, and moments turn into memories.

Established in 2025, Oxford Sofas began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create sofas that combine timeless design, lasting comfort, and uncompromising quality. Over time, that idea grew into a complete range of seating solutions for homes, offices, hotels, and commercial interiors.
