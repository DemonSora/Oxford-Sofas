About Oxford Sofas

Oxford Sofas is a sofa-focused brand offering both in-house manufactured designs and a curated selection of sofas from trusted partner brands. Our collection is built to serve modern homes, offices, hotels, and commercial interiors.

Founded in 2025, Oxford Sofas began with a clear purpose: to create and offer sofas that feel right in real spaces. Over time, this approach expanded, combining our own manufacturing with carefully chosen external brands that meet the same standards we apply to our own products.

Designed With Purpose

Every Oxford Sofa, whether produced in-house or sourced from a partner brand. Is selected or designed to balance comfort, proportion, and durability.

From frame construction to upholstery, each piece is evaluated for everyday use, long-term performance, and visual balance within a space.

We offer sofas in multiple types, sizes, and configurations, ensuring they work naturally in settings ranging from private living rooms to professional waiting areas and hospitality environments.

Quality, Simplified

For our own manufactured sofas, we use carefully selected materials, strong internal structures, and refined finishes to ensure consistency and longevity.

For external brands, we apply the same standards. Assessing materials, construction quality, and overall build before including them in our collection.

Nothing excessive. Nothing unnecessary.

Just well-made sofas, built or chosen to last.

Thoughtfully Curated

Oxford Sofas is not about endless choice. It's about considered selection.

Alongside our in-house designs, we collaborate with established manufacturers and brands whose products align with our expectations for comfort, reliability, and design integrity. Every sofa earns its place through performance, not trend.
